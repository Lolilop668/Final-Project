Dataset: AirBnB Listings in Major US Cities (Deloitte ML Competition)

Download link (Kaggle):
https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/rudymizrahi/airbnb-listings-in-major-us-cities-deloitte-ml

How to get the data:
1. Open the link above (a free Kaggle account is required to download).
2. Download both files: "train.csv" and "test.csv".
3. Place them in the same folder as the notebook (or in a "data/" subfolder).
4. Run the notebook from top to bottom.

Details:
- Files used: train.csv and test.csv
- train.csv: 74,111 rows, 29 columns, includes the target log_price
- test.csv: 25,458 rows, 28 columns, no target (used only for predictions)
- Target variable: log_price (natural logarithm of the nightly price in USD)
- Cities: New York, Los Angeles, San Francisco, Washington DC, Chicago, Boston
- License / usage: public dataset, free for educational and research use
  under the terms shown on the Kaggle page.

Note: the full dataset is not uploaded to this repository because of its size.
Use the link above to download it.
